02

PASSION

  • DESIGN

    My love for design sprouted by unending imaginations of how fellow designers' graphic contents could look better than they actually did in real life. At times I spend hours on Pinterest consuming design content from fellow designers and commenting on one or two things portrayed in their designs. To me, design is not only my bread but also a satisfying realm.

  • TECHNOLOGY

    Working in FE, flowing with tech is a necessity. Tech is my skill sharpener and that is why I love it. Making software and designing awesome tools sells my capability and enables me to earn some social approval. Tech-related designs have some authority over ordinary designs for their psychological ingredients that keep viewers glued. The world is turning smart and that's the reason I not only love but work with tech.

  • STORY

    Story helps me communicate my inner thoughts, worries, ideas, and passion. From telling a story I learn a lot from what others think of me and my work. From the UX principle of "a design is 90% better of when tested" telling a story is key in self-testing as it brings some commonality and differences on board. Story is a source of constructive feedback and new ideas.
